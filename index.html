Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Sipience is a local-first hydration tracker. Your logs, profile, and chats live on your device. This policy explains what data the app touches and how.

1 Data we collect locally

  • Your profile — name, weight, height, age, gender, activity level. Entered by you during onboarding, stored on-device in an encrypted Room database.
  • Hydration logs — beverage type, volume, timestamp, created when you log a drink.
  • Activity events — detected walking, running, and cycling durations, generated by Android's Activity Recognition API.
  • App settings — reminder times, quiet hours, preferences, stored in Android DataStore.
  • Optional profile photo — stored locally in the app's private files directory.
  • Optional AI chat history — conversations with the Sipience AI Coach, stored locally.

All of the above stays on your device. We do not run a server. We do not have accounts. We do not upload your logs anywhere.

2 Permissions we use

  • Location (coarse) — to fetch local weather from Open-Meteo so we can dynamically adjust your hydration goal. Coordinates are sent to Open-Meteo and discarded.
  • Activity recognition — for passive walking / running / cycling detection to adjust your goal.
  • Notifications — to show reminder alarms you configure.
  • Exact alarms — so scheduled reminders fire on time.

3 Third-party services

  • Open-Meteo (api.open-meteo.com) — receives only your current latitude / longitude to return weather. No account, no identifier.
  • Google Gemini (generativelanguage.googleapis.com) — when you chat with the AI Coach, your current hydration context and message are sent to Gemini to generate a response. Google's Gemini API policy applies. We do not persist prompts on any server we control.
  • Google Play Services — standard Android APIs (Activity Recognition + Location) provided by Google.

4 Data you can export / delete

  • Settings → Export Data (CSV) gives you the full hydration log as a CSV you can share.
  • Settings → Delete Drinking Logs removes all logs.
  • Settings → Reset Profile wipes ALL Sipience data (logs, profile, reminders, customs, chats, achievements, photos) and returns you to onboarding.
  • Uninstalling the app removes everything.

5 Children

Sipience is intended for users 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.
